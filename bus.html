Stop

The goal of this project was to create a new bus stop for Northwestern University. Emphasis was placed on tapping into the style of design all across the Evanston campus. I began by thumbnailing some ideas, and creating some working drawings based on them.

From here I decided on a style that was inspired by the newest undergraduate buildings on campus - Mudd Library and Kresge Hall - and looked to include things students waiting for the bus could benefit from. These took the form of outlets, overhead covering to protect from the weather and an alert to buses approaching. The final product is shown below.
