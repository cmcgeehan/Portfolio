CS + Social Good

Last year, I felt out of place in the computer science department at Northwestern. I could not see how the work I was doing would lead to making any real impact in the lives of others. So I sought out to design a student group where students could come together and use the skills they were aquiring through their studies to help social impact groups solve their technology-based problems.

Our Mission

To ignite a new generation of leaders working at the intersection of technology and social impact, expand the use of technology as a force for good, and build a more socially responsible tech industry.
