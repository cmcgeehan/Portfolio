Float

The goal for this project was to develop a piece of footwear in which we were interested. I personally have always disliked the way boxing shoes look, so I decided to make a boxing shoe that I would be happy to wear, without losing any functionality. These were my original thumbnails.

I then decided I wanted to style this shoe so it could be used by the Evanston Boxing Club. I was very interested in the idea of moving away from laces towards mesh with straps, since in my experience athletes will over-tighten laces and damage their feet. This led me to my final design, shown below.
