Sturdy

For our first project, this industrial design class was tasked with creating a new box cutter. The intended user was a construction worker. I started by obtaining a standard boxcutter and sketching it

From here, I branched off, thinking of the ways to improve upon this box cutter for someone who works in construction. These ideas included making the box cutter sturdier, allowing it to attach to a toolbelt and allowing for extra blades to be stored within. The final product is below.
